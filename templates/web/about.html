What we do

Mission : We secure and transform the future of orphan and poor children using technology.

What problem are we solving?

NGO’s and Govt. bodies working for welfare of poor or orphaned children are right now only providing education and support till intermediate level. In most of the cases these children are not able to get higher education or employment due to the lack of professional training and expertise. Resources used on these children goes to waste because they once again return to homelessness, poverty and unemployment.

Orphanages, NGOs and Govt. Institutions are providing basic standard education to these children till intermediate school level. Govt. schemes are provided to support higher education of these children, but only a few of them are able to secure their future.

What is our solution approach

We offer a solution in which orphan and poor children will get education / training of professional software, web development, app development, software engineering, marketing, sales and other professional courses to prepare them according to industry level expertise

Professional courses on software, marketing and entrepreneurship will allow these unprivileged children to have a part-time or freelance job while continuing their higher studies. Entrepreneurship courses can inspire them to innovate and take risks and provide solutions to the world’s largest problems.

Our Programs & Services

Life Skills & Creative Education

We provide life skills & creative education to the unprivilidged children. This will help them become a better decision maker, leader and elite personality. Education is provided on no-time bound manner.

Virtual Classes

We setup digital labs acrosss our centers where anyone interested student can study online through video courses or they can be taught by an available vounteer teacher. This will provide opportunity to the remote poor students.

Clothes & Shoes Giving

As an environmental issue, we recycle the old clothes and shoes, so that these clothes can be used by the needy people. In future we are also planning to refurbish shoes, and then they will be distributed.

Professional Services

We provide professional service like - Web Development, App Development and Software Development services to our clients for a minimum fees. We also provide consultancy in the field of virtual classes.

Who are we?

We are a not-for-profit social enterprise which is working in the remote areas to help the poor children to pursue their respective interests. We are professionals in different fields like engineering, computer science and computing, who have common interest to tackle the problem of lack of creative and life skills education to the children who are not good at grads. We help the poor grade students as well poor and higher grades students to help them achieve more through introducing them to technology. The only problem we see is the lack of instrastructure and money. So we provide digital libraries to help students to learn themselves through the knowledge of web. Other projects including sports training, instrastructure and employment will be our main focused projects.

.
What we do?

  1. We provide digital libraries facilities in a particular center, where computers with internet connectivity and web cameras facilities are installed. We provide quality education through our virtual classroom technology to the children who are excellent or average on studies. Our virtual class technology will be available 18 hours a day, which is divided in slots. Our volunteer teachers from across top education institutes students, professionals and businessman signup for volunteer teacher positions.

  2. In our coming years we will setup sports instrastructure where children will be professionally trained to become our future sports champions. We are also planning to open low cost residential facility to our sports children so that they can focus more on their training.

Whom do we benefit and How?

  1. Since we provide our facilities to the unprivileged and poor students in terms of grads as well as financials, we provide our services to every needy students and we do not distinguish based upon religion, caste and creed.

  2. We will students who are good in sports to become our future champions, so our volunteer sports persons will provide training to our students, beneficiary students to sports and other programs will be selected solely on interest based. We charge a minimum fee to the students for every year. In case fees are not available to the family we exempt their annual fees.

  3. Beneficiary students will have to return their dues to our organization once are earning well in their lives. In case of non-employment of our student we do not charge our dues.
